### ⚡ Quick Summary

|Metric|Focus|Formula|When Important|
|---|---|---|---|
|**Accuracy**|Overall correctness|(TP + TN) / (TP + TN + FP + FN)|Balanced datasets|
|**Precision**|Correctness of positive predictions|TP / (TP + FP)|Avoiding false alarms|
|**Recall**|Ability to find all positives|TP / (TP + FN)|Avoiding missed detections|
|**F1 Score**|Balance of both|2 × (Prec × Rec) / (Prec + Rec)|Imbalanced datasets|
